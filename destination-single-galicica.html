National Park Galichica 9.4 Rating

Galichica is a mountain situated across the border between North Macedonia and Albania. There is a national park on the Macedonian side of the mountain, situated between the two biggest lakes in the republic: Lake Ohrid and Lake Prespa. It stretches over an area of 227 square kilometres (88 sq mi).
